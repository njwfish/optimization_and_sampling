After the second-order and linear-solver material, we return to the first-order update template and remove two different simplifying assumptions from the basic deterministic story.
Stochastic optimization keeps the same descent geometry while replacing exact gradients with noisy ones.
Constrained optimization keeps exact derivatives but restricts which directions are feasible.
Both themes will reappear later once momentum, augmentation, and sampling enter the picture.
